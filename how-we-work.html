The method

Three layers.
One operating partner.

We do not run AI workshops. We do not sell you a course. We sit with you, get inside how your business actually works, and install AI as a system. Across your day, your team, your whole company.

How an engagement runs.

01

Map your universe (Week 1)

We sit with you for two to three sessions. We learn how your business wins, where the money comes from, where you spend time, what only you can do, what should never have been on your plate. We document everything in a private workspace.

02

Plug AI in, surgically (Week 2)

Every workflow we mapped becomes a candidate for an AI agent, automation, system or model. We score them by impact and build a 90-day roadmap. You see exactly what gets built, in what order, and what changes for you.

03

Build, run, manage (Week 3 onwards)

We do not hand you a deck. We build the systems, run them with you, train your team, and stay on as your fractional AI partner. Most clients keep us on a monthly retainer because the AI stack is never done.
