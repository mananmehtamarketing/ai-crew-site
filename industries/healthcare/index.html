Frequently asked

Questions doctors & clinics ask us.

add Is AI safe to use in a clinical setting?

Yes, when scoped correctly. We deploy AI only for documentation, scheduling, intake, follow-ups, and search. Clinical decisions stay with the clinician, always. Every output is reviewed before it leaves.

add Will the AI scribe replace my EMR?

No. It feeds into it. The AI listens to the consult and drafts the SOAP note in your structure. You review, sign, and it lands in your existing EMR or chart.

add How does AI help with WhatsApp patient inquiries?

An AI assistant trained on your clinic's tone and FAQs handles 70-80% of inbound (booking, rescheduling, simple post-op questions, directions). Anything clinical or urgent is escalated to a human.

add Is patient data safe?

Yes. We deploy in compliance-aware setups: data stays inside your accounts, sensitive info is masked, and we follow Indian DPDP Act and HIPAA-equivalent practices for exports.

add How much admin time do clinicians actually save?

Most clinicians get 3-5 hours back daily after week 3. Documentation drops from ~90 minutes a day to under 20.
