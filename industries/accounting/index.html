Frequently asked

Questions cas & accountants ask us.

add Can AI handle Indian GST returns end-to-end?

Yes, in supervised mode. AI pulls invoice data, generates GSTR-1 / 3B drafts, validates against rules, and flags mismatches. A CA always reviews and files.

add Will AI replace my junior team?

No. It removes the manual matching and chasing. Juniors graduate faster to advisory work. Most firms add 15-30% more clients without hiring.

add Is client financial data safe?

Yes. Data stays in your accounts. No third-party training. Full audit trail of every AI action.

add How fast does month-end close speed up?

From 7-10 days to 2-3 days for most mid-sized firms within 60 days of going live.

add Does the AI work with our existing accounting software?

Yes. We integrate with Tally, Zoho Books, QuickBooks, BUSY, Marg, SAP, and most major Indian and global stacks via API or file sync.
