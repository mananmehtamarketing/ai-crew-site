The story

Why AI Crew exists.

I started in restaurants. Built and ran them. Got burnt out. Walked away and started MarketinCrew, a marketing agency that grew into a 50-person team, shipping work for 300+ brands across India, Europe and North America.

Two years ago I became obsessed with AI. Not the hype. The actual transformation it ran on my own day. Email triaged. Meetings prepped. Content drafted. Decisions sharper. The hours I worked went down. The output went up. The quality went up.

So I started installing AI inside my agency. Outreach systems. Content engines. Internal tools. Custom GPTs for every team. And then I started doing the same for clients. They were marketing clients to begin with, but the work I was doing for them was no longer marketing. It was AI.

Founders kept asking me, almost weekly, the same thing. "Can you do this for me, in my business?" That question kept landing. So I made it a real thing.

That is AI Crew. A separate practice for founders and high-earning professionals who do not have time to figure AI out themselves, and do not want a generic SaaS plug-in. They want it built for them, run for them, and managed by someone with skin in the game.

Four things I believe.

person
