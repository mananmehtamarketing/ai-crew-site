The founder

Hi, I'm Manan.
I run AI Crew.

Restaurateur turned marketer turned AI operator. I have spent the last decade building businesses, and the last two years installing AI inside them.

The short story.

I started in restaurants. Built and ran them. Got burnt out. Walked away and built MarketinCrew, a marketing agency now 50 people strong, 80+ brands shipped across India, Europe and North America.

Two years ago, I started installing AI into our own agency. Outreach, content, ops, internal tools. The output of the team went up. The hours we worked went down. The quality went up.

Founders started asking me, almost weekly, the same thing. "Can you do this for me, in my business?" That conversation kept happening. So I made it a real thing.

That is AI Crew. A one-to-one AI consultancy for founders and high-earning professionals who do not have the time to figure this out themselves and do not want a generic plug-and-play SaaS. They want it built for them, run for them, and managed by someone who has skin in the game.

That is what we do.
