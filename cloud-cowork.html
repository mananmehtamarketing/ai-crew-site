Good questions

Things founders ask us

Do I need to be technical? add

No. This is built for operators and founders, not developers. There is no terminal and no code. If you can use a folder on your laptop, you can use this. We do the setup and teach you the simple way to delegate work to it.

Is it safe to give an AI access to my files? add

We never hand it the run of your whole computer. We set it up in one connected workspace with clear boundaries, and it shows you its plan before it touches anything. You approve, then it works. You stay in control the whole time.

What is not included in the $250? add

The price covers our setup, teaching and ongoing support: the monthly group call, your private 90-minute call with Manan, the WhatsApp group and your assistant. It does not include third-party subscriptions, such as your own Claude plan or any other tools you choose to connect.

How quickly will it be running? add

The core setup happens right after your fit call. Most founders have their workspace connected and their first dashboards running within the first week, then we keep tuning it on the monthly calls.

Is there a contract? add

No long lock-in. It is a flat monthly subscription you can pause or stop. We would rather keep earning it each month than tie you down.
